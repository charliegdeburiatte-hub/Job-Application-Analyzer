# Privacy Policy for Job Application Analyzer

**Last updated:** January 19, 2026

## Overview

Job Application Analyzer is designed with privacy as our top priority. Your data never leaves your device.

## Data Collection and Storage

### What We Collect:
**Nothing.** We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal data to external servers.

### What Stays on Your Device:
All of the following data is stored **locally** in your browser's storage only:

- Your uploaded CV/resume document
- Parsed CV profile (skills, experience, education)
- Job analysis results and match scores
- Application history and tracking data
- Extension settings and preferences

### What We DON'T Collect:
- No analytics or tracking
- No telemetry or usage statistics
- No personal information transmission
- No cookies or third-party tracking
- No server-side storage of any kind

## How Your Data is Used

1. **CV Analysis:**
   - Your CV is processed entirely in your browser
   - Used only to compare against job postings
   - Never sent to external servers

2. **Job Matching:**
   - Job descriptions are read from the current page
   - Compared locally against your CV
   - Match scores calculated in your browser

3. **Application Tracking:**
   - Your application history is stored locally
   - Used only for your personal reference
   - Never shared or transmitted

## Permissions Explained

The extension requests the following permissions and here's why:

### storage
- **Purpose:** Save your CV, job history, and settings locally in browser storage
- **Data stored:** CV document, parsed profile, analyzed jobs, user preferences
- **Location:** Your browser's local storage only

### activeTab
- **Purpose:** Read job posting content from the current tab
- **When used:** Only when you click the extension icon on a job page
- **Data accessed:** Job title, description, company name, location
- **What we do:** Extract job details for local analysis only

### scripting
- **Purpose:** Inject content scripts to detect job pages automatically
- **When used:** Only on LinkedIn, Indeed, and Reed job pages
- **What it does:** Detects when you're viewing a job posting

### notifications
- **Purpose:** Show optional match score notifications
- **When used:** Only if you enable notifications in settings
- **What it shows:** Match percentage for analyzed jobs

### Host Permissions (linkedin.com, indeed.com, reed.co.uk)
- **Purpose:** Access job posting content on these specific sites
- **Limitation:** Only works on job pages, not your entire browsing
- **What we access:** Job description text for local analysis

## Third-Party Services

**None.** This extension does not use any third-party services, APIs, or external servers.

- No external API calls
- No cloud storage
- No analytics services
- No advertising networks
- No tracking scripts

## Data Security

### Local Storage Security:
- All data stored using browser's built-in storage API
- Protected by browser's security mechanisms
- Isolated from other extensions and websites

### Data Transmission:
- **None.** Zero data transmission to external servers
- Job pages are read locally, never sent elsewhere
- Your CV never leaves your device

## Data Retention and Deletion

### How Long We Keep Data:
- Data is kept indefinitely in your browser storage
- You control all data retention

### How to Delete Your Data:

**Option 1 - Clear specific data:**
1. Open extension settings
2. Click "Clear CV Data" to delete your CV
3. Click "Clear Job History" to delete analyzed jobs

**Option 2 - Delete all extension data:**
1. Right-click extension icon
2. Select "Manage Extension"
3. Click "Remove"
4. Confirm deletion

**Option 3 - Browser storage:**
1. Firefox Settings → Privacy & Security
2. Cookies and Site Data → Manage Data
3. Search for "Job Application Analyzer"
4. Remove
